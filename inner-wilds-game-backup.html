Mining
Click to capture mouse. WASD move. Shift sprints. Hold left-click to mine the crosshair block, or tap monsters. Water cannot be mined. Right-click places carried blocks. Mine blocks to fill the quickbar. Space jumps/swims. E talks/uses. F steadies. M menu. R cycles carried blocks. [ / ] adjust look speed. V toggles view. 1-4 select slots.

Field Notes

The world is not a lecture. It is a machine for making choices visible.

Strategy

  • Noise attracts Hollowlings. Sprinting, panic swings, and reckless digging raise it.
  • Animals trust quiet movement. Approach with low Noise and press E.
  • Waystones react to dialogue choices, not just answers.
  • Your quickbar starts empty. Mine moss, stone, amber, or mirror before you can place it.
  • Hold left-click to mine. Harder blocks take longer. Water is liquid, not a block resource.
  • Amber blocks are strong. Mirror blocks calm an area. Moss blocks are cheap and useful.
