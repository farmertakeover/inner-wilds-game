Field Notes

You wake on a broken survey island. Restore the waystones, recover the lost chart, and learn why the Hollowlings were left to guard the ruins.

Controls

  • Hold left-click to mine. Harder blocks take longer.
  • Water is liquid - swim through it, don't try to mine it.
  • Press C to open crafting. Make swords, bows, tools, and building materials.
  • Equip weapons in the hotbar. Left-click attacks, right-click aims bow.
  • Press M anytime to open the menu and adjust settings.

Tips

  • Your hotbar starts empty. Mine blocks to collect them.
  • Hunger drains faster while sprinting or swimming. Cold nights and water lower Temp.
  • Weather changes visibility and survival pressure: fog quiets Noise, mirror rain cools you.
  • Hollowlings sleep beside old survey markers. They wake when attacked, not on a timer.
  • The Cartographer can explain the island's route. Find the blue cloak near the center.
  • Animals trust quiet movement. Approach with low Noise and press E.
  • Adjust render distance and look speed in Settings for best performance.
